## Full description (max 4000 characters)
BookOrbit is a native Android client for your own self-hosted BookOrbit
server — a personal library for ebooks and audiobooks that you control.

BookOrbit does not host any content itself. Point the app at your own
BookOrbit server and get a fast, native way to browse, read, and listen
to your library on your phone or tablet.

FEATURES
• Secure sign-in to your server via OIDC
• Browse and search your full library, with smart scopes and collections
• Author and series pages
• Built-in EPUB reader
• Audiobook player with background playback and Android Auto support
• Download books for offline reading and listening
• Your data stays on your server — no ads, no tracking, no analytics

REQUIREMENTS
You need access to a running BookOrbit server to use this app. See
https://github.com/bookorbit/bookorbit for server setup.
